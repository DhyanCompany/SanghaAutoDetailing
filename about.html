About Sangha Auto Detailing

Passion for perfection, delivered to your doorstep.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sangha Auto Detailing began as a dream to bring professional-grade car care directly to vehicle owners in Ontario and Alberta. What started with a single van and a passion for perfection has grown into a trusted mobile detailing service with hundreds of satisfied customers.

We believe every car tells a story, and we're here to make that story shine. From daily drivers to exotic collectibles, we treat every vehicle with the same level of care and attention to detail.

## 🚀 How to Activate Email Functionality

### Quick Setup (2 Steps):
1. **Sign up at EmailJS**: https://www.emailjs.com/ (free)
2. **Update Public Key**: Replace `YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE` in `js/script.js` with your key

**See EMAILJS_SETUP_GUIDE.md for detailed instructions!**
